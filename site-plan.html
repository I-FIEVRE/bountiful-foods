Purpose

The purpose of the Bountiful Foods website is to provide information about the company and its entertaining activities, and the conditions for good practice.

Target Audience


Audience

The general public is made up of people between the ages of 20 and 60. They are either medium or small business entrepreneurs or future entrepreneurs. The audience is made up of both men and women regardless of their marital status. Likewise, it includes both Italians and immigrants because the region is rich in migration from different horizons (Europe, Latin America, Africa, and Asia).


Personas


Creator and Manager

Fictional name: Maria Rossi

Demographics: 50 years old, married with 2 children

Goals and tasks: She would like to meet other entrepreneurs who work like her a lot with the internet and have information to grow her business.

Environment: She has her own store in town and online. She works with her daughter. Most of its customers are online. She uses the internet on a daily basis and responds to client emails.


Future Entrepeneur

Fictional name: Luis Sanchez

Demographics: 26 years old, single

Goals and tasks: He arrived from Peru in Italy 8 years ago. He has done a lot of work and now wants to start his own business thanks to the savings he has made. He wants to get information, help, and meet people who have gone through or are going through the same journey as him.

Environment: He has a mobile phone and a good quality internet subscription. He surfs the internet very well.
